FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can LABC mobilize a project team?
For most projects, LABC can mobilize a core team within 2–4 weeks of contract award. For urgent requirements, we maintain a roster of pre-qualified professionals who can be deployed faster. Contact us to discuss your specific timeline needs.
Do you serve projects outside Saudi Arabia?
Yes. While our primary focus is Saudi Arabia, LABC has delivered projects across the GCC (UAE, Kuwait, Bahrain, Qatar, Oman) and in select international markets. We have experience navigating multi-jurisdictional regulatory requirements.
What does a typical project engagement look like?
Every engagement begins with a detailed scope and needs assessment. From there, we propose the most appropriate delivery model — whether that's full EPCM, standalone engineering, PMC, or advisory. We provide a detailed proposal with scope, deliverables, schedule, and fee structure.
Is LABC on Saudi Aramco's Approved Vendor List?
Yes. LABC is a registered and approved engineering consultant in Saudi Aramco's Vendor Master, as well as being registered with SABIC, SWCC, NWC, and numerous other major clients. We can provide registration certificates upon request.
How is LABC's pricing structured?
We offer flexible fee structures depending on the project type: lump-sum fixed fee, time-and-materials, or a hybrid model. For PMC engagements, we typically work on a percentage of project cost or a fixed monthly retainer. We are always transparent about our fee basis.
