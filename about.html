Who We Are

A Legacy of
Engineering Excellence

Founded in 2003, LABC has grown from a regional consultancy into a recognized leader in multi-disciplinary engineering and project management services across Saudi Arabia and the broader GCC region.

We serve clients across oil & gas, petrochemical, power, water, infrastructure, and commercial sectors — delivering complex projects with precision, on time, and within budget.
